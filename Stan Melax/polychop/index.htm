Simple Polygon Reduction

  

Although not as relevant today as it was back in the 90s, to maintain a good frame rate in 3D interactive programs it often necessary render objects using fewer polygons.  Here you will find a simple technique that is fairly fast, and generates reasonably good low-poly models.

Look at the Polygon Reduction article from the November '98 issue of Game Developer Magazine which explains how this polygon reduction technique works.
